Introduction

I have been employed as a PhD candidate since September 2018. Although my background is in physics and radiometrics, my current work focuses on models for nanoscale frictional phenomena of layered materials and drug resistance in chronic myeloid leukaemia treatments. This involves numerical simulations, data analysis and mathematical derivations. For more info, please explore my science page or send me an email.
