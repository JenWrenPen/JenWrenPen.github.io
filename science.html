Research and publications

After completing a master's degree in the instrumentation and modelling of the measurement of nuclear radiation, I went on to complete and defend a PhD. My doctoral work spanned two projects: thermal effects on the frictional properties of layered materials and modelling drug resistance in chronic myeloid leukaemia treatment. If you have any questions, you can contact me at science@jennifer.sh.
