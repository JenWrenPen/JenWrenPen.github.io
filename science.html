Research and publications

After completing a master's degree in the instrumentation and modelling of the measurement of nuclear radiation, I am studying for a PhD in statistical mechanics. My doctoral work spans three projects: thermal effects on the frictional properties of layered materials; modelling drug resistance in chronic myeloid leukaemia; and extreme value statistics. If you have any questions, you can contact me at science@jennifer.sh.
