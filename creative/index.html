Creative projects

Peruse a sample of my artistic outputs using different media.

Paint & Pens

Doodling and sketching have been my favourite boredom-busters for as long as I can remember. When I was about 12 years old, my art teacher introduced me to acrylic paints - they quickly became my favourite. Since then, I casually produce art as gifts using either paints or marker pens. My favourite pieces to make are miniature works that are only a few inches wide and "improvements" to secondhand artworks.

Sewing projects

Most of my sewing skills are put towards fixing or tailoring clothes, but occasionally I will feel insprired to make something special.

LEGO™ designs

I've designing LEGO™ builds digitally for a couple of decades, and recently I've extended these skills into scientific models for demonstration.
