Scientific Research and Publications

After completing a master's degree in the instrumentation and modelling of the measurement of nuclear radiation, I went on to complete and defend a PhD. My doctoral work spanned two projects: thermal effects on the frictional properties of layered materials and modelling drug resistance in chronic myeloid leukaemia treatment. If you have any questions or wish to collaborate, you can contact me at science@jennifer.sh.
